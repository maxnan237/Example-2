## Research Framework

Each company is analyzed through the same lens:

1. **Business Overview** — segments, customers, dependencies
2. **Industry Scan** — market size, growth, competitors, regulation
3. **Financial Deep Dive** — multi-year statements, accounting flags
4. **Moat Analysis** — 5 moat sources rated, trajectory, summary
5. **Management Review** — leadership, capital allocation, insiders
6. **Valuation Check** — multiples, peer comps, Bull/Base/Bear scenarios
7. **Bear Case** — thesis killer, structural weaknesses, impairment scenarios
8. **Earnings** — most recent quarter vs. guidance
9. **Decision Log** — watch list with alert thresholds
10. **Investment Memo** — 4-5 page synthesized summary + PDF

All factual claims sourced to filings. External data flagged. No training-data filling.
