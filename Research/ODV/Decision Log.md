# Osisko Development (ODV) — Decision Log

*Initiated: 2026-04-12*

## Investment Thesis Summary

ODV is a Canadian pre-production gold miner developing the Cariboo Gold Project in British Columbia. Fully permitted, partially financed, with 2.07 Moz probable reserves at a projected AISC of US$1,157/oz. First gold expected 2027-2028. Trades at ~$3.30 USD / ~$4.65 CAD with ~$1.4B CAD market cap. **Gold at US$4,750/oz creates massive economic upside IF execution works**.

**Bull thesis:** Permitted, advanced-stage project in stable jurisdiction with exceptional gold prices creating 2-3x P/NAV discount. Acquisition optionality from gold majors. Sean Roosen's Canadian Malartic track record.

**Bear thesis:** Going concern warning, FID not yet declared, 262% dilution in 27 months, 5% perpetual NSR to OR Royalties, Xatśūll First Nation opposition, ground conditions worse than planned, single-asset concentration, management <1% aggregate ownership.

## Decision Framework

### To buy at ~US$3.30 / C$4.65:

1. Conviction gold stays above US$3,500/oz
2. Willingness to accept additional dilution (10-20% more expected)
3. Patient time horizon (2-3 years to first gold, 4-5 years to steady production)
4. Small position sizing (<2% of portfolio)
5. View that Cariboo gets built (or bought out)
6. Tolerance for going concern / binary outcomes

### To avoid:

1. Concern about gold price peaking here
2. Unwillingness to accept going concern companies
3. Focus on near-term earnings
4. Unwillingness to tolerate -70% downside scenarios
5. View that junior gold developers are structurally broken business model
6. Preference for diversified / producing gold exposure (NEM, GOLD, AEM, AU ETFs)

### Current stance: **Speculative only — small position sizing required**

ODV is a **high-conviction gold development bet with genuine binary risk**. The potential for 2-3x returns (if FID + build + gold stays) is real. So is the potential for -70% losses (if execution slips OR gold corrects).

**This is not a core holding.** Appropriate only for:
- Investors with pre-existing gold exposure thesis
- Those comfortable sizing 1-2% positions
- Those willing to accept complete loss of capital

**Key triggers for position changes:**

**Buy more:** FID declaration + gold stays above US$4,000
**Hold:** Construction progressing + cash position stable
**Trim:** Gold falls below US$3,500 without construction progress
**Exit:** Going concern becomes worse; another missed safety incident; Xatśūll wins legal action
