# Osisko Development Corp (ODV) — Business Overview

*Analysis date: 2026-04-12 | Sources: FY2025 40-F + AIF (ex991), MD&A (ex993), Financials (ex992)*
*Note: ODV is a Canadian filer using Form 40-F; reports in CAD under IFRS. Fiscal year ends December 31.*

---

## 1. What the Company Does

Osisko Development is a **pre-production Canadian gold mining company** developing the Cariboo Gold Project in British Columbia as its flagship — and only material — asset. Spun out from OR Royalties (formerly Osisko Gold Royalties) in November 2020 via reverse takeover of Barolo Ventures Corp.

[ODV_FY2025_ex991.htm, AIF — General]:
> "Osisko Development is a Canadian-based exploration and development company focused on past-producing properties located in mining friendly jurisdictions with district scale potential. The Company's objective is to become a continental North American intermediate precious metals producer, through the development of its fully permitted flagship Cariboo Gold Project."

- **Stage:** Development (not producing)
- **Tickers:** NYSE American (ODV), TSX-V (ODV)
- **HQ:** Montreal, Quebec
- **Employees:** ~102 (Dec 31, 2025)
- **Shares outstanding (Mar 27, 2026):** 304,596,755 (fully diluted: 428,155,642)

## 2. Key Projects

### Cariboo Gold Project — FLAGSHIP, SOLE MATERIAL PROPERTY (100% owned)

[ODV_FY2025_ex991.htm, Schedule C]:
> "The Cariboo Gold Project is in the historic Wells-Barkerville mining camp of British Columbia [...] ODV's land holdings consist of 384 mineral titles totaling 142,885.12 hectares."

- Located in Wells, BC (74 km east of Quesnel, ~500 km north of Vancouver)
- Historic Cariboo Gold Rush district — producing since 1860s
- Underground gold project; long-hole longitudinal retreat mining
- Multiple deposit zones: Cow, Valley, Shaft, Mosquito, Lowhee, BC Vein, KL, Bonanza Ledge
- **FULLY PERMITTED** as of Q4 2024
- Construction Manager: JDS Energy & Mining Inc. (engaged Feb 9, 2026)

### Tintic Project (Utah) — Non-Material, Care & Maintenance

1,370 claims in East Tintic Mining District. Acquired 2022 for US$156.6M. Small-scale Trixie test mine produced 3,970 oz gold in FY2025 generating $35.5M revenue. No current exploration; company evaluating options.

### San Antonio (Mexico) — DIVESTED January 2026

Sold to Axo Copper Corp for 15.3M Axo shares + contingent milestone payments (up to US$4M). Reclassified as discontinued operations in FY2025.

### Electric Elements Mining Corp — 43.2% Equity Interest

Minority position in James Bay region exploration asset (Quebec).

## 4. Construction Status (as of March 2026)

[ODV_FY2025_ex993.htm, Section 6.1]:

### What's done:
- 2.1 km of underground development from Cow Portal to Lowhee Zone
- Site camp expanded to 266 rooms
- Water treatment plant commissioning (Q2 2026)
- Waste rock storage facility + sediment control pond: underway
- JDS Energy & Mining engaged as construction manager

### What's NOT done:
- **Final Investment Decision (FID) NOT YET DECLARED** — the key unlock for remaining US$350M Appian facility
- Full construction not commenced
- Transmission line (69/138 kV) — permits expected H1 2026

### Red flags:
- "Development progress has been below plan while encountering challenging ground conditions in and around the Lowhee fault, requiring enhanced ground support"
- **January 22, 2026 contractor fatality** — site suspended until March 2, 2026 (~5 weeks lost)
- Original H2 2027 first gold timeline likely to slip to 2028+

## 9. Key Dependencies

1. **Gold price** — every US$100/oz move changes NPV by ~C$140M after-tax (Cariboo 1.9 Moz total production)
2. **FID execution** — must declare before remaining US$350M Appian draws available
3. **Capital markets access** — going concern means ODV needs continued equity raises through 2027
4. **Xatśūll resolution** — unresolved legal risk could halt construction
5. **Ground conditions** — Lowhee fault has been worse than expected
6. **Contractor execution** — JDS manages the build; performance critical
7. **BC regulatory environment** — stable but complex First Nations consultation requirements
