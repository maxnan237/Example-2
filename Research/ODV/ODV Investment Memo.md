# ODV Investment Memo

**Osisko Development Corp (NYSE: ODV, TSX-V: ODV) | April 12, 2026**

---

## The Business in 30 Seconds

Osisko Development is a Canadian pre-production gold miner developing the Cariboo Gold Project in British Columbia — its sole material asset. 2.07 Moz probable reserves + 3.48 Moz additional M&I and inferred resources. Fully permitted (Q4 2024). Construction in early stages; first gold targeted H2 2027 (likely 2028 given delays). Single-asset concentration. Spun out from OR Royalties (Osisko Gold Royalties) in 2020 via reverse takeover. Led by Sean Roosen (built Canadian Malartic, sold for C$3.9B in 2014). Stock ~US$3.30 / C$4.65, market cap ~$1B USD / C$1.4B.

---

## What's Going Right

**Gold is at US$4,750/oz — almost 2x the Feasibility Study base case.** At current spot prices, Cariboo's after-tax NPV likely exceeds C$4-5B vs. the $943M FS base case. This is a generational gold price environment.

**Cariboo is fully permitted.** Environmental Assessment Certificate (Oct 2023), Mines Act Permits (Nov 2024), Environmental Management Act Permits (Dec 2024). "Shovel-ready." Permits are arguably irreplaceable — new BC gold mine permits now take 4+ years and C$50-100M to obtain.

**Project economics are robust.** FS base case: C$943M NPV, 22.1% IRR, US$1,157/oz AISC (2nd quartile), 10-year mine life at 190 koz/yr. Spot case (US$3,300/oz): C$2.07B NPV, 38% IRR.

**Strong reserve/resource base.** 2.07 Moz probable reserves at 3.62 g/t (above industry average). 5.54 Moz total endowment including inferred. Active drilling (160,000m in 2026) could expand this.

**Major financing secured.** US$450M Appian Capital facility (July 2025). US$100M drawn; remaining US$350M available upon Final Investment Decision. Pro-forma cash ~C$640M.

**Acquisition optionality.** Agnico Eagle, Barrick, Kinross, Newmont could acquire ODV post-FID. Sean Roosen's Canadian Malartic precedent suggests an exit is the endgame.

**Jurisdiction is stable.** British Columbia is Tier-1 mining jurisdiction with rule of law, infrastructure, skilled labor.

---

## What Could Go Wrong

**Going concern warning in FY2025 auditor's opinion.** Company states current cash + Appian availability will NOT be enough to reach first gold. More equity raises needed.

**FID NOT yet declared.** 18+ months late vs. original 2025 FS timeline. Construction can't fully ramp without FID. Every quarter of delay extends timeline and adds cost.

**Massive dilution.** Share count up 262% in 27 months (84M → 305M). Fully diluted 428M. Likely 15-25% more dilution before first gold.

**5% NSR perpetual to OR Royalties.** ~C$292M LOM royalty drag. No buyback right. Permanent economic haircut.

**Xatśūll First Nation opposition.** One of three affected nations has not signed benefits agreement; threatening judicial review of permits. Could delay construction 12-24 months.

**Ground conditions worse than anticipated.** "Development progress has been below plan while encountering challenging ground conditions" at Lowhee fault. Could worsen at depth.

**Contractor fatality (January 2026).** Site suspended 5-6 weeks. Safety record impaired.

**Capex overrun risk.** Mining project construction typically overruns 20-40%. A 25% overrun on C$881M = C$220M additional funding need.

**Gold price could correct.** Current US$4,750/oz is historically extreme. A decline to US$3,000/oz (still elevated historically) would compress NPV by 40%.

**Management has <1% insider ownership.** Limited personal alignment with shareholders on dilution decisions.

**Combined CEO + Chairman role** (Sean Roosen). Concentrated authority.

## Decision

**Current stance: Speculative only — small position sizing required.**

ODV is a **high-conviction leveraged bet** on three outcomes happening together:
1. Gold prices staying elevated (US$3,500+/oz)
2. Successful construction on roughly planned budget
3. Continued access to capital markets through FID + build

If all three work: **stock could 2-3x over 2-3 years** (FID + first gold + potential acquisition).

If any single vector breaks: **severe downside (-40% to -70%)**.

The 262% dilution in 27 months, going concern warning, FID delay, perpetual 5% NSR to OR Royalties, Xatśūll opposition, fatality, and ground conditions issues make this a genuinely high-risk profile. The bull case requires a string of favorable outcomes.

**Appropriate for:**
- Investors with pre-existing gold exposure thesis
- Those comfortable with 1-2% position sizing and potential complete loss
- Patient investors (2-3 year minimum time horizon)
- Those who view ODV as a call option on gold + Cariboo build

**Not appropriate for:**
- Core portfolio holdings
- Income-focused investors (no dividend)
- Anyone unwilling to accept 70%+ drawdown scenarios
- Those preferring diversified gold exposure (NEM, GOLD, AEM)
