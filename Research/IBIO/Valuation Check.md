## Scenario Valuation Framework

### Bull Case: Clean Phase 1 + Pharma Partnership

- IBIO-600 Phase 1 PK confirms 2-4x/year dosing; safety clean
- IBIO-610 IND filed early 2027; positive preclinical reinforces class
- Pharma partner licenses IBIO-600 globally: $50M+ upfront, ~$1B+ total deal value
- No further major dilution through 2027-2028

**Valuation anchors:**
- Comparable Phase 1 obesity antibody partnership deals (Altimmune, Terns, Skye) have priced $80-250M EV on Phase 1 data
- Assume $200M EV = fully diluted price of ~$1.30 (if 195M shares) or ~$6 (if only current 34.5M)
- Most likely outcome: **$5-8/share** if base-diluted; **$3-5/share** fully-diluted

**Base case: Fair value ~$5-6/share. Return: +125% to +170%.**

### Base Case: Mixed Phase 1 + Continued Funding Cycle

- IBIO-600 shows acceptable but unexceptional PK; requires Phase 1b extension for POC
- Biohaven Phase 2 data (2H 2026) confirms mechanism class value but iBio is still behind
- Another capital raise required by 2028; ~20-40M additional shares issued at $1.50-$3
- Oncology programs still unpartnered

**Valuation anchors:**
- EV stays in $10-50M range
- Stock price remains in **$1.50-$3** range through 2027-2028 data readouts

**Base case: Fair value ~$2-3/share. Return: -10% to +35%.**

### Bear Case: IBIO-600 Safety Signal or Class Failure

- Phase 1 shows safety concerns (immunogenicity, muscle hypertrophy adverse events)
- OR Biohaven BHVN Phase 2 data (2H 2026) shows myostatin pathway fails to deliver lean-mass preservation in obesity
- OR Regeneron's COURAGE Phase 2 data reveals class-wide safety issues
- Cash runway extended to 2028 but pipeline optionality collapses

**Valuation anchors:**
- Stock trades below net cash (~$1.50/share) — market prices in expected destruction of cash via wind-down
- Further dilutive raise at $1-$1.50 → post-raise stock $0.75-$1.20

**Bear case: Fair value ~$0.75-$1.50/share. Return: -33% to -66%.**

### Worst Case: Dilutive Survival + Going Concern Re-Emerges

- Phase 1 data read-out delayed; no partnership interest; cash depletes faster than projected (current $9M/Q burn trending higher)
- By late 2027, new capital raise needed at severely depressed prices
- Third reverse stock split
- Stock trades in $0.30-$0.80 range

**Worst case: Fair value ~$0.30-$0.70/share. Return: -70% to -85%.**

## What Would Make the Valuation Compelling

- **Partnership deal announcement**: any credible Big Pharma licensing deal for IBIO-600 with meaningful upfront ($30M+) would validate the pipeline EV well above current levels
- **Clean Phase 1 safety data**: first-in-human data from Australia expected late 2026 / early 2027 — de-risks the lead asset
- **Positive Biohaven BHVN Phase 2 topline data (2H 2026)**: validates the myostatin obesity mechanism, raising the asset-class ceiling
- **EV to go negative** (stock below net cash, ~$2.10/share): creates a near-pure option on pipeline success with cash backstop — the most asymmetric entry point

---

## Decision

With the stock near net cash and the pipeline not yet validated, iBio offers **modest expected return with extreme variance**. Current stance is best characterized as **"Speculative — watchlist for catalyst entry"**:
- Not attractive at $2.22 as a long-term investment
- Becomes meaningfully attractive below $2.00 (near or below net cash)
- Becomes asymmetrically attractive if IBIO-600 Phase 1 safety is clean
- Becomes uninvestable if the first-patient dosing is delayed, or if comparable readouts from Biohaven/Scholar Rock disappoint
