## Research Framework

Each company is analyzed through the same lens:

1. **Business Overview** — What the company does, segments, customers, dependencies
2. **Industry Scan** — Market size, growth, competitors, regulatory environment
3. **Financial Deep Dive** — Multi-year P&L, cash flow, balance sheet, accounting flags
4. **Moat Analysis** — Switching costs, network effects, cost advantages, intangibles, efficient scale
5. **Management Review** — Leadership, capital allocation, insider activity, guidance credibility
6. **Valuation Check** — Multiples, peer comps, scenario framework (Bull / Base / Bear)
7. **Bear Case** — Thesis killer, structural weaknesses, assumptions at risk, impairment scenarios
8. **Earnings** — Most recent quarter analysis (guidance vs. reality, KPI trends)
9. **Decision Log** — Watch list with alert thresholds, decision framework
10. **Investment Memo** — 4-5 page synthesized summary + PDF

All factual claims are sourced to SEC 10-K filings or flagged as external data. No training-data filling.
