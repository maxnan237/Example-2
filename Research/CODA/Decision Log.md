# Coda Octopus Group (CODA) — Decision Log

*Initiated: 2026-04-12*

---

## Investment Thesis Summary

CODA is a $138M market-cap underwater technology micro-cap with a unique real-time 3D volumetric sonar (Echoscope) and a US Navy-adopted Diver Augmented Vision Display (DAVD). Debt-free with $28.7M cash, growing revenue 30%+ (partly acquisition-driven), but trades at premium multiples (33x P/E) with extreme illiquidity (6M share float).

**Bull thesis:** Unique technology (no direct competitor in real-time 3D sonar), DAVD scaling with US Navy and SOCOM, Echoscope PIPE NANO Gen positioning for UUV sensors ($5.3B DoD autonomy budget), debt-free balance sheet with M&A firepower, potential acquisition target for Teledyne/Kongsberg.

**Bear thesis:** Tiny company competing against giants (Teledyne, Kongsberg), extreme illiquidity, thin analyst coverage (1), governance concerns (combined CEO/Chairman), organic growth modest (~5% ex-acquisitions), customer concentration in Services segment, premium valuation vulnerable to execution missteps.

## Decision Framework

**To buy at ~$12.20:**
- Conviction that DAVD scales to $8-15M over 3 years
- Belief that Echoscope NANO Gen captures UUV sensor share
- View that Teledyne/Kongsberg could acquire at premium
- Willingness to accept extreme illiquidity and thin float volatility
- Patient time horizon (3-5 years)

**To avoid:**
- Concerned about competitive vulnerability to Teledyne/Kongsberg
- Unwilling to accept micro-cap illiquidity risk
- Governance concerns about combined CEO/Chairman structure
- Valuation (33x P/E) seems rich for $27M revenue business
- Prefer dividends/buybacks over retained capital

**Current stance: Speculative interest.** The company has a genuinely differentiated technology with real defense adoption, but the premium valuation + illiquidity + micro-cap governance concerns make this a high-risk, high-conviction thesis. Size position accordingly — **not a core portfolio holding**.

**Key triggers:**
- DAVD ANU completion for untethered variant (Q2 FY2026) — binary catalyst
- Another acquisition announcement — size, price, strategic fit
- Any M&A interest from Teledyne/Kongsberg (strategic buyer premium)
- DAVD deployment expansion — new Navy commands, European Navy
- Organic growth acceleration in Q3-Q4 FY2026

**Key risks:**
- Sondergaard continued block sales pressuring thin float
- DAVD revenue miss (lumpy timing)
- Dilutive acquisition
- Teledyne launching competing 3D sonar
- US defense CRs extending into FY2026
