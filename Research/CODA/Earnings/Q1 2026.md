## Section 5: What Actually Changed?

### Materially improved
- **Marine Technology segment +47%** — Echoscope equipment sales driving growth
- **Operating leverage working** — op margin expanded 240 bps
- **Asia market strong** — +63% in the region
- **Product diversification via PAL** — 24% of revenue vs. 0% in Q1 FY2025

### Materially deteriorated
- **Tax rate headwind** — EPS flat despite operating income +53%
- **No new acquisition announced** — management telegraphed intent but nothing executed
- **No explicit commentary on DAVD ANU progress** — binary catalyst remains pending

### What is new
- First full quarter with PAL fully integrated
- Echoscope PIPE NANO Gen Series beginning customer shipments (launched Sept 2025)
- Strong Asian regional growth emerging as trend

### What did NOT change despite management emphasis
- **DAVD revenue lumpiness** — Q1 contribution modest; back-ended to Q3-Q4
- **Dividend/buyback** — still no capital return
- **Governance structure** — combined CEO/Chairman, no Lead Independent Director

---

## Section 6: Stock Reaction

Stock reaction to Q1 print was modest. The stock had rallied from $5.76 to $17.28 over the prior 52 weeks; Q1 print occurred with stock in pullback mode at ~$12. Earnings beat was largely expected given strong Q4 FY2025 results.

| Dimension | Rating |

The business is executing on strategy but remains heavily dependent on DAVD scaling (lumpy, Q3-Q4 concentrated) and another acquisition to sustain growth optics. **Next catalyst: Q2 FY2026 earnings (~June 2026) and any M&A announcement.**
