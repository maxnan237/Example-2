# Coda Octopus Group (CODA) — Business Overview

*Analysis date: 2026-04-12 | Primary source: FY2024 10-K (fiscal year ends Oct 31) + FY2025 10-K/A*

---

## 1. What the Company Does

Coda Octopus is a small-cap underwater technology company specializing in **real-time 3D volumetric imaging sonar** (Echoscope®), **Diver Augmented Vision Display (DAVD)** for the US Navy, and defense engineering services. Products serve commercial subsea (offshore wind, oil & gas, ports) and defense markets.

[CODA_10K_FY2024.htm, Item 1]:
> "Coda Octopus Group, Inc., through its wholly owned subsidiaries, operates... Our Marine Technology Business is a technology solution provider to the underwater market. It owns key proprietary and unique technology comprising its real time volumetric imaging sonar technology (Echoscope® technology) and diving technology (Diver Augmented Vision Display - 'DAVD')."

**Corporate history:** Formed from Coda Technologies (UK, 1994, Heriot-Watt University spinoff). Reverse merger into US-listed Panda Project in July 2004. Key acquisitions: Omnitech (Norway, 2002 — Echoscope technology), Martech (UK, 2006), Colmek (Utah, 2007), PAL (UK, October 2024).

**103 employees worldwide** as of Jan 2025 filing date (pre-PAL addition). HQ: Orlando, FL with major operations in Edinburgh (Scotland), Salt Lake City, Dorset (UK), Copenhagen.

## 2. Business Segments (Three as of FY2025)

### 1. Marine Technology Business ("Products Segment") — $13.2M revenue

Commercial sonar and diving technology for subsea markets.

**Key products:**
- **Echoscope® / Echoscope PIPE® 3D/4D/5D/6D sonar** — real-time volumetric imaging in zero-visibility water
- **DAVD** — heads-up display for US Navy divers (sonar imagery through helmet)
- **F280 Series®** — GNSS-aided inertial navigation
- **Echoscope PIPE NANO Gen Series®** — ultra-compact sonar for UUVs (launched Sept 2025)
- **GeoSurvey®, Survey Engine®, DA4G®** — geophysical survey software

### 2. Acoustic Sensors & Materials (PAL) — $5.4M revenue (NEW in FY2025)

Precision Acoustics Limited (UK), acquired October 29, 2024 for $6.5M net. Specializes in ultrasonic hydrophones and acoustic materials for medical imaging and non-destructive testing.

### 3. Defense Engineering Services ("Services Segment") — $7.9M revenue

Colmek (Salt Lake City) + Martech (UK) — sole-source sub-assembly supplier to major defense programs.

[CODA_10K_FY2024.htm, Item 1 — Colmek]:
> "Colmek is the sole source for the parts that they supply to these programs. This business model ensures recurring and long tail revenues since we continue to supply parts, typically for the life of the program, which can span decades."

Named programs: **Raytheon's Close-In Weapon Support (CIWS)** and **Northrop Grumman's Mine Hunting Systems Program (AQS-24)**.

## 3. Revenue Model

Three revenue types across segments:

**Equipment sales** — largest at $14.4M (54%) in FY2025. Echoscope systems, DAVD, navigation equipment, PAL hydrophones.

**Equipment rentals** — $1.5M (5.6%) in FY2025, down 62% YoY as US offshore wind pullback reduced rental demand.

**Engineering parts & services** — $6.4M (24%) from Colmek/Martech sub-assemblies on long-running defense programs.

**Software sales & services** — $4.3M (16%). Echoscope USE, CMS software, post-installation support.

### Revenue recognition
- **Point-in-time** (equipment sales): $9.3M FY2024
- **Over time** (engineering percentage-of-completion): $11.0M FY2024

## 7. Key Dependencies

### US Navy / DoD funding

DAVD revenue of $3.7M in FY2025 (+208% YoY) is dependent on US Navy procurement cycles. Continuing resolutions in FY2024 caused US revenue to decline 20%.

### Prime defense contractor relationships

Services segment depends on Raytheon CIWS and Northrop AQS-24 programs. Loss of either would be material (20-30% of revenue collectively in any given year).

### Offshore wind market

US offshore wind pullback (Trump administration policy shift) caused rental revenue to decline 62% in FY2025. Partial offset from oil & gas recovery and Echoscope equipment sales.

### Export controls

> "Our real time 3D sonars which are depth rated above 300 meters along with our inertial navigation and attitude measurement sensors (F280® series) are subject to export control for certain countries, including China."

### Single-site manufacturing concentration

Echoscope manufactured primarily in Edinburgh (21K sq ft owned). DAVD assembly in Orlando. Any site disruption would be material.

### Currency risk

66.6% foreign revenue, assets held in GBP/DKK/EUR. No hedging. FX can materially impact reported USD results.
