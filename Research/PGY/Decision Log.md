# Pagaya Technologies (PGY) — Decision Log

*Initiated: 2026-04-10 | Based on FY2025 10-K and supporting documents*

---

## Investment Thesis Summary

Pagaya is an AI lending technology platform that just achieved its first GAAP profitable year ($81M) after burning cash since IPO. The stock has been crushed (-73% from highs) on a Q1 2026 guidance miss, creating a potential deep value opportunity at ~4x forward earnings — or a value trap if a credit cycle materializes.

**Bull thesis:** Asset-light AI lending platform with expanding take rates (3.2% → 4.9%), first GAAP profitability, $225M FCF, 100% partner retention, and 8/8 analyst Buy ratings — all at forward P/E of ~4x. The market is pricing in a credit crisis that may not come.

**Bear thesis:** AI untested through credit cycle, $504M in retained credit exposure, $824M in high-cost debt (8.875%), Network Volume growth decelerating to +3%, Q1 2026 guidance miss, and "true lender" regulatory risk. The stock is cheap for a reason.

## Decision Framework

**To become a buyer at ~$12:**
- Q1 2026 results come in at or above $335M (high end of guide)
- Consumer delinquencies remain stable through mid-2026
- FRLPC% holds at 4.5%+
- No adverse regulatory developments (CFPB, "true lender")
- Management provides more granular partner diversification data

**To avoid (stay on sidelines):**
- Q1 2026 at low end ($315M) or below
- Investment losses spike in any quarter
- ABS market tightens (spread widening, volume pullback)
- Further dilution through equity issuance
- New regulatory action targeting AI lending

**Current stance: Speculative interest.** The valuation is compelling (4x forward P/E, 8/8 Buy) if you believe credit conditions hold and execution continues. But the credit cycle risk is real, the AI is untested through a downturn, and the Q1 guidance miss raises questions about near-term momentum. This is a high-conviction, high-risk position — not a core holding. Appropriate only for investors with credit cycle views.
